May 29th, 2019: Output interactive GEO maps.

Created by: (Team Atlas) Nancy Sardar, Juan Linares, Jacob Weekley, Alex Smyntyna, Fahim (Luke) Choudhury

About: Team Atlas is creating a Digital Mapping website to visualize information associated with locations of the Northeast Metro Atlanta region around Georgia Gwinnett College (GGC). This map must be interactive and accessible to everyone, including the GGC community members, through the GGC website. Team Atlas is one of two teams working on this project. Atlas is responsible for the output portion of this project. The map will consist of an interactive display for users to absorb information intuitively, with unique symbols defined by a map legend. By clicking on data points, the user sees information for each location in a small or expanded window. With location data gathered by GGC students, the website shall be a convenient tool that provides location specifics. The goal of Team Atlas is to provide this area’s information efficiently within an aesthetic, interactive map.
